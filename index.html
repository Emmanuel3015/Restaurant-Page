Manuel's Bites

Appetizers

  • Buffalo Chicken Wings

    A classic American appetizer made with chicken wings, hot sauce, and butter, often served with celery sticks and blue cheese dressing.

  • Nacchos

    A Mexican-inspired appetizer made with tortilla chips, melted cheese, jalapenos, and various toppings such as salsa, guacamole, and sour cream.

  • Meat Balls

    Small, bite-sized balls of meat, often made with beef, pork, or a combination of the two, and served with a tangy sauce.

Main Courses

  • Rositto

    A creamy Italian rice dish that can be flavored with various ingredients such as mushrooms, seafood, or meat.

  • Tacos

    A traditional Mexican dish consisting of a corn tortilla filled with various ingredients like meat, cheese, lettuce, and tomatoes.

  • Chicken Parmesan

    Fresh salmon, bread, and drizzled with sweet sauce.

Desserts

  • Banana Pudding

    A Southern favorite, consisting of a layered mix of sweet banana pudding, fresh bananas, Nilla wafer cookies, and whipped cream.

  • Apple Pie

    A traditional American dessert, often considered patriotic, made with a combination of apples such as Granny Smith and Honeycrisp for a balance of sweet and tart flavors.

  • Chocolate chip cookies

    A classic American cookie, invented by Ruth Graves Wakefield in 1930, made with semi-sweet chocolate chips and a crispy exterior.
